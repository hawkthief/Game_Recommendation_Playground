# {{Quality}}

## Definition
What aspects of perceived excellence are present in the game
scalar on:
How much and how effective is the polish on the game

## Additional Notes
Meant to interact with a tolerance threshold instead of a desire metric. Games will not necessarily be prioritized based on polish but a game with a badly polished aspect should not be recommended to someone with a lower tolerance for it.
